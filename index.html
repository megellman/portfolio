About Me

Megan Ellman holds an Undergraduate Degree in Communications and Minor in Community Engagement Writing from the University of California, Santa Barbara. For the past two years, she worked as an Onboarding Coordinator for ClickUp, a productivity platform.

It was here that she developed a passion for coding and web development. Currently, she is enrolled in the Full Stack Flex Bootcamp at the University of California, Berkeley where she is learning HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

When she is not writing code, Megan loves ocean swimming, hiking, or taking her dog Molly to the park.
